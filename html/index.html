About us

Welcome to 10XMA, where we elevate the standards in modeling and acting. In a fiercely competitive industry, we provide aspiring models and actors with a unique edge through comprehensive training and strategic guidance. Our mission is to empower you with the necessary tools and knowledge to navigate and succeed in the modeling and acting industries.

Who we are

Based in Toronto, 10XMA focuses on the Canadian and New York markets. Our academy offers tenfold strategies and intensive training, pushing you to achieve unparalleled success. We nurture talent and equip passionate individuals with the skills needed to excel. Despite our primary focus on Canada and New York, our work often takes us to various markets, providing diverse opportunities for growth.

Our goal

We aim to help you realize your dreams by providing expert training and mentorship. Our approach ensures that you are well-prepared to face the industry's challenges, armed with the knowledge and skills required for a successful career.






Meet Yash

Yash, our lead coach, brings over a decade of industry experience. He has faced and overcome numerous challenges, shaping his approach to training and mentorship. Yash's expertise includes accent classes, voice acting, acting, and modeling. He leverages his extensive learning from coaches and mentors to provide a comprehensive training program covering all industry aspects. His goal is to ensure emerging talents avoid the pitfalls he encountered, using his experience to guide and shape young talent.

"I often get asked by aspiring models how to break into and navigate this competitive industry. Spending hours explaining the intricacies made me realize how much young talent needs guidance. Many are in the same situation I was in a few years ago, which motivated me to mentor aspiring models and actors. Agencies often don’t have the time to nurture talent as they compete with other agencies, and models compete with each other. If you think you can succeed just because you’re attractive, know that you’re entering a fiercely competitive market and might be quickly overlooked. I encourage those who are genuinely passionate about their craft to pursue this industry. If your passion isn't at least a nine out of ten, it might not be the right path for you, as it can lead to financial loss and heartbreak. For those truly passionate, I'm here to help ensure you're entering the industry for the right reasons. Once you commit, there should be no looking back.” - Yash
