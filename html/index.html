About Us

Welcome to 10XMA, where we elevate the standards in modeling and acting. In a fiercely competitive industry, we provide aspiring models and actors with a unique edge through comprehensive training and strategic guidance. Our mission is to empower you with the necessary tools and knowledge to navigate and succeed in the modeling and acting industries.






Who we are

Based in Toronto, 10XMA focuses on the Canadian and New York markets. Our academy offers tenfold strategies and intensive training, pushing you to achieve unparalleled success. We nurture talent and equip passionate individuals with the skills needed to excel. Despite our primary focus on Canada and New York, our work often takes us to various markets, providing diverse opportunities for growth.






Our goal

We aim to help you realize your dreams by providing expert training and mentorship. Our approach ensures that you are well-prepared to face the industry's challenges, armed with the knowledge and skills required for a successful career.
